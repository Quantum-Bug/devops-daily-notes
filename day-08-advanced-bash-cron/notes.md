### Functions

### Cron Jobs
Used to schedule tasks automatically.

Format:
* * * * * command

| Field | Meaning |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Minute |
| 2 | Hour |
| 3 | Day |
| 4 | Month |
| 5 | Day of week |
